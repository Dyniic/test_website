I had forgotten how hard it is to do simple day to day stuff. Even in London, a city that does not exactly withhold modern conveniences, 5 hours to find UPS, we were clueless about finding Wi-Fi, and we can forget about eating 3 square meals a day. I have yet to take a show, brush my teeth, change my clothes since December 14th.
