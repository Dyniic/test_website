Transport

Getting to and from the wedding

The roads on the estate are a bit rough and quite steep. We can traverse them in our little Fiat 500, so most of your should manage, but if you have a super low riding car, we wouldn't recommend it.

There should be enough parking for everyone, but if you can car-pool we're less likely to have a grid-lock situation.

If you're an Uber user you'll be pleased to hear the operate in the Constantia/Hout Bay area. That said, please bear in mind that there are likely to be droves of guests leaving at roughly the same time and Constantia Nek is not a likely spot for a cabby to be hanging out. You might well run into a supply problem. Again, please team-up with others if you can.
