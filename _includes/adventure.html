Adventure

Team Last Responders aims to make an impact all around the world. To date, Last Responders organized ambulance donations to Asia, Afria, and South America.

  • 2010

    London to Mongolia

    The Team Last Responder's inaugural ambulance donation covered the insane distance of 10,000 miles from London, United Kingdon to Ulan Bator, Mongolia over 6 weeks. Last Responders was one of 400 team participating in the Mongol Rally and raised monday for Mercycorp and MSF.

  • 2011

    London to Mali

    On December 23rd, 2011 Team Last Responders embarked on its second adventure charity project as a participant of the Timbuktu Challenge. This challenge covered the distance of 6000 miles, starting in London and ending in West Africa, Mali. For this project, the team partnered with Mali Health Organizing Project and the Salif Keita Global Foundation.

  • 2013

    New York to Peru

    Our latest project was our longest and most ambitious - 16,000 miles from New York to Trujillo, Peru. This 4 month project had us partnering with Arizona based Esperanca and Vive Peru.
