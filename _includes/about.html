About

We are a nonprofit dedicated to increasing awareness about environmental issues and combating climate change.

Rallies and Events

In order to raise awareness, we focus on organizing events throughout the United States, that range from city cleanups to informational workshops.

Social Media

Our largest platform to increase awareness is social media. Go follow our Instagram!.

Community Projects

In order to enact change in communities around the world, we create initiatives to plant gardens and increase eco-friendly products.


Our Mission


Our mission to create a comprehensive framework from which solutions and actions can be taken to prevent climate change from continuing to impact our world.
