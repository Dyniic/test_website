Adventure

Team Last Responders aims to make an impact all around the world. To date, Last Responders organized ambulance donations to Asia, Afria, and South America.

  • July 2010

    Mongol Rally

    The Team Last Responder's inaugural ambulance donation covered the insane distance of 10,000 miles from London, United Kingdon to Ulan Bator, Mongolia. Last Responders was one of 400 team participating in the Mongol Rally and raised monday for Mercycorp and MSF.

  • December 2011

    Timbuktu Challenge

    The second charity project took the team through to Western Africa where the team donated an ambulance to a hospital in Mali.
